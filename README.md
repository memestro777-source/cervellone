🧠 Big Brain!
A fast-paced brain-training game. Test yourself across four categories — Visual, Logic, Math, and Memory — earn a brain size in cm³, and beat your record. Single HTML file, zero dependencies.
How to Play
Pick a mode: 🎲 Random challenge (a surprise minigame each stage) or 🎯 Pick your challenges (you choose category and game). Play 4 stages of 45 seconds each. Correct answers build a 🔥 streak bonus; mistakes cost points. At the end you get your brain size, a brain type, and a report card with your strongest and weakest category.
The 16 Minigames

👁️ Visual: Cube Counter, Missing Piece, Bubbles, Odd One Out
🧩 Logic: The Gears, Hexagon Path, The Balance, Symbol Sudoku
➗ Math: Missing Operators, The Number Plate, Clock Difference, Calculation Chain
💭 Memory: Flip & Find, Shape Order, Action Sequence, Memory Grid

Features

Invisible adaptive difficulty: challenges scale up as you answer well and ease off when you struggle; each category remembers your progress
Balanced scoring across all games, with a streak multiplier
🏆 Persistent best score and games played
🔊 Fully synthesized sound (Web Audio API), with mute toggle
🌍 English and Italian, switchable with the 🇬🇧/🇮🇹 flags
📱 Single-screen layout that fits any device, no scrolling

Running It
Open cervellone.html in any modern browser. No server, no installation, works offline.
